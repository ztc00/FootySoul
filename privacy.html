Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

Footy Soul (“we”, “us”, or “our”) operates the Footy Soul mobile application. This page explains how we collect, use, and protect your information.

1. Information we collect

  • Account information: name, email address, and optional profile details.
  • Authentication data: sign-in provider information (Apple, Google, email).
  • Usage data: games you join or create, venues, and in-app actions.
  • Location data: approximate location to help you find nearby games (where permitted).
  • Payment data: processed securely by our payment provider. We do not store full card numbers on our servers.

2. How we use your information

  • To create and manage your Footy Soul account.
  • To let you discover, join, and organise games.
  • To send notifications about game updates and reminders.
  • To improve the app and prevent fraud or abuse.

3. Sharing

We do not sell your personal data. We share limited information with:

  • Service providers (e.g. Supabase, Stripe, analytics tools) to operate the app.
  • Authorities where required by law.

4. Data retention

We retain your data for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide the service, comply with legal obligations, or resolve disputes.

5. Your rights

You can request access, correction, or deletion of your personal data at any time by contacting us.
